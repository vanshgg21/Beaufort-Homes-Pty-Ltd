Who We Are

The trade's scaffolding supplier, by the pallet or the piece.

BBN Group is a Sydney scaffolding wholesaler. From our Smithfield warehouse we sell the systems and components that keep sites moving: forged and pressed couplers, quick lock scaffolding, jacks and base plates, props, steel boards and a full range of site supplies.

Scaffolders, builders and formworkers buy from us because the gear is right, the price is wholesale and the answer on the phone is straight. Tell us what you need and when you need it, and we will have it ready.
