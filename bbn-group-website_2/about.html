About BBN Group

Good gear, wholesale prices, and a straight answer.

Our Company

BBN Group is the trading name of BBN Universal Pty Ltd, a scaffolding wholesaler operating from our warehouse at 29/323 Woodpark Road, Smithfield, in Sydney's west.

We sell the full spread of access and support equipment: forged and pressed steel couplers, quick lock scaffolding systems, jacks and base plates, props and column clamps, steel boards, and the fencing and consumables that keep a site running.

Our customers are scaffolders, builders and formworkers across Greater Sydney, buying by the piece, the pallet or the truckload.

How We Work

Wholesale is a simple business done properly. We keep the gear in stock, we price it fairly, and we tell you straight what is available and when you can have it.

Send an enquiry through the website with the items and quantities you need, or pick up the phone. Either way you deal with people who know the product, and collection from Smithfield or delivery to site can usually be arranged quickly.

Leadership

Sushil Gupta
Managing Director

Sushil leads BBN Group day to day and has built the business on long-standing relationships with scaffolders and builders across Sydney. He also leads the group's construction and land businesses, which keeps the scaffolding operation close to the sites that use it.

The BBN Group

BBN Group sits alongside two sister companies: Beaufort Homes, our construction and development arm, and BBN Developers, our land company. Together the group covers land, building and the equipment that builds.
